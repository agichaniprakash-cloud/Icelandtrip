About this trip

This trip is all about family, fun, and the magical Aurora Borealis. From Iceland’s glaciers to Norway’s Arctic skies, we’ll explore, laugh, and make memories together.

Our Day-by-Day Plan

  • 27 Oct: ✈️ Flight DEL → Reykjavik (via Munich & Zurich). Arrive and check in at Airbnb, Reykjavík (3 nights).
  • 30 Oct: 🏨 Move to Seljalandsfoss Horizons (2 nights).
  • 1 Nov: 🏨 Stay at Hotel Berg, Keflavik (1 night).
  • 2 Nov: ✈️ Reykjavik → Oslo → Leknes. 🏨 Stay in Ramberg, Lofoten (3 nights). 🌌 First chance for Northern Lights!
  • 5 Nov: ✈️ Flight Leknes → Tromsø. 🏨 Airbnb Tromsø (3 nights). 🌌 Best spot for Aurora adventures.
  • 8 Nov: ✈️ Return journey from Tromsø.

✨ Highlight nights for the Northern Lights: Lofoten & Tromsø 🌌
