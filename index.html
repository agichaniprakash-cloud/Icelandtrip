About this trip

Sneha & Babita, this journey is our gift of time together. From Iceland’s glaciers to Norway’s Arctic skies, we’ll explore, laugh, and create unforgettable memories under the Aurora Borealis 🌌.

Our Day-by-Day Plan

  • 27 Oct ✈️ Flight: Delhi → Reykjavik (via Munich & Zurich). Arrive in Iceland.
  • 27–30 Oct 🏨 Stay: Airbnb – Hrefnugata 8, Reykjavík (3 nights).
  • 30 Oct – 1 Nov 🏨 Stay: Seljalandsfoss Horizons (2 nights).
  • 1 Nov – 2 Nov 🏨 Stay: Hotel Berg ★★★★★, Keflavik (1 night).
  • 2 Nov ✈️ Flights: Reykjavik → Oslo → Leknes (via Bodø).
  • 2–5 Nov 🏡 Stay: Home in Ramberg, Lofoten (3 nights). 🌌 Northern Lights chance!
  • 5 Nov ✈️ Flight: Leknes → Tromsø (Widerøe Airlines).
  • 5–8 Nov 🏡 Stay: Airbnb in Tromsø (3 nights). 🌌 Best Aurora views!
  • 8 Nov ✈️ Return from Tromsø.

✨ Highlight Nights for Northern Lights: Lofoten & Tromsø 🌌
