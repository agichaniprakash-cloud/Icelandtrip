🇮🇸 Iceland — Places to Go

From geothermal baths to glacial lagoons and dramatic waterfalls — here are the top places and experiences we've planned.

Blue Lagoon

Geothermal spa — relaxing soak in mineral-rich water.

Golden Circle

Thingvellir, Geysir, Gullfoss — classic loop of natural highlights.

Glacier Lagoon (Jökulsárlón)

Boat tours among floating icebergs — unforgettable.

Seljalandsfoss & Skógafoss

Iconic waterfalls — walk behind Seljalandsfoss if weather allows.

More Iceland experiences

  • Sky Lagoon (Reykjavík thermal lagoon)
  • Whale Watching (seasonal near Reykjavik / North Iceland)
  • Caves & basalt formations (sea & lava caves)
  • Black sand beaches & coastal cliffs
  • Reykjavík highlights: Hallgrímskirkja, Harpa

🏡 Lofoten — Places to Go & Experiences

Dramatic fjords, photogenic fishing villages, white-sand beaches and remote hikes — perfect for photography and Aurora nights.

Reine

Classic fishing village — base for boat rides and winter kayaking.

Haukland Beach

Famous white sand & turquoise water views — beautiful walks.

Kvalvika Beach

Hike-in beach with dramatic cliffs.

Henningsvær

Art galleries and the famous rock football pitch.

Complete Lofoten Places

  • Reine — village, boat rides, winter kayaking
  • Haukland Beach
  • Kvalvika Beach
  • Henningsvær
  • Orcas — seasonal whale watching
  • Hamnøy — iconic rorbuer & photo spot
  • Reinebringen — steep hike with panoramic view
  • Ryten — scenic hike to overlook Kvalvika
  • Mannen — popular local hike
  • Eggum — sunset & viewpoints
  • Trollfjord Cruise
  • Holdøya — offbeat coastal viewpoint
  • Svolvær — hub town for galleries and excursions
