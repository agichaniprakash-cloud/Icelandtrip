🇮🇸 Iceland — Places to Go

Blue Lagoon, Golden Circle, Seljalandsfoss, Skógafoss, Jökulsárlón and more.

🏊 Blue Lagoon

Relax in geothermal spa — mineral-rich waters.

🗺 Golden Circle

Thingvellir, Geysir, Gullfoss — classic Iceland loop.

🧊 Glacier Lagoon (Jökulsárlón)

Floating icebergs and black sand beaches nearby.

🕳 Caves & Basalt

Explore sea caves and volcanic formations.

🏡 Lofoten — Places to Go

Ramberg, Reine, Haukland, Kvalvika and more — dramatic fjords & fishing villages.

Reine

Iconic fishing village — base for boat rides & winter kayaking.

Haukland Beach

White sand & turquoise water — beautiful walks.

Kvalvika Beach

Hike-in beach with dramatic cliffs.

Henningsvær

Art galleries and the famous football field on the skerry.

More places in Lofoten

  • Orcas (seasonal whale watching)
  • Hamnøy — iconic rorbuer (photo spot)
  • Reinebringen hike (panoramic view)
  • Ryten & Mannen hikes
  • Eggum — sunsets & viewpoints
  • Trollfjord cruise & Holdøya
  • Svolvær — town & galleries

🌌 Tromsø — Highlights & Places

Arctic Cathedral, Fjellheisen cable car, fjord cruises, and world-class Northern Lights tours.

Fjellheisen Cable Car

Panoramic views over Tromsø and the fjords.

Arctic Cathedral & City

City life, cafes, museums, and the iconic church.

Other Tromsø experiences

  • Northern Lights Tours
  • Nordic Sauna at Pust
  • Sledding & winter activities
  • Magic Bar (local nightlife)
  • Whale watching excursions (seasonal)
